Hwiyeon Yoo

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University (SNU), South Korea. My research interests include Embodied Navigation AI, Computer Vision, Visual Navigation, Vision-based Semantic Perception, and Robotics. Prior to my Ph.D. studies received B.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Seoul National University in 2017.
